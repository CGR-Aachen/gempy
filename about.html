About us¶

Computational Geology RWTH Aachen University.¶

We investigate novel methods to integrate geoscientific data and knowledge in process simulations of subsurface flow and transport problems with a specific focus on inverse problems, data fusion, and the analysis and quantification of uncertainties. The institute is lead by Prof. Florian Wellmann.
